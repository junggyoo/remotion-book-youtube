## Visual Language

- **Shape Language:** geometric (systematic, structured content)
- **Texture Mood:** grain
- **Layout Bias:** centered
- **Motion Character:** precise

## Visual Metaphors

### 1. Compound Growth

- **Metaphor:** Exponential curve rising from flat baseline
- **Usage:** keyInsight scenes -- 1% compound effect visualization

### 2. Identity Iceberg

- **Metaphor:** Iceberg with visible tip (outcome) and hidden mass (identity)
- **Usage:** keyInsight identity scene -- identity vs outcome contrast

### 3. Habit Loop

- **Metaphor:** Circular 4-step cycle with directional arrows
- **Usage:** framework scene -- 4 Laws circular structure
